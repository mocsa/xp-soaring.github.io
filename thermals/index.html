Thermals

Objective

Soaring requires either ridge lift or thermals to stay aloft. We want thermals to have appropriate strength, have a life-cycle, have cumulus clouds, relate to ground features.

Current status

X-Plane has built-in thermals, which I've not yet tested at all. They are not currently related to cumulus clouds.

Alex Ferrer has done some development for X-Plane thermals also.
