Welcome to Day 7, the final day of the Krasnoff County Regionals!

Today's task is a 420km triangle, with legs of 108, 112, 125 and 75 km.

The best weather of the competition! Conditions are good with light winds and 4.5 knot climbs to 5000 feet until 4:30pm, and steady deterioration after that.

To begin, 'Hunt' for a thermal, and select 'Start Task' at the top of a climb when you're ready to go. There is more help and a detailed discussion of the simulator in the help file.
