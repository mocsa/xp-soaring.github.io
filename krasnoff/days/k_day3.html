Welcome to Day 3 of the Krasnoff County Regionals!

Today's task is a 270km triangle, with legs of 110, 95 and 65 km.

The day is not particularly strong, with average 3.5 knot thermals. Conditions will weaken rapidly from 14:30, and by 15:00 conditions are expected to be weak and blue, with climbs to 3500 foot max.
