Welcome to Day 4 of the Krasnoff County Regionals!

Today's task is a 275km triangle Gransden-Edgehill-HusBos-Gransden, with legs of 125, 85 and 65 km.

A windy day, and the conditions are mixed. Conditions are good for streeting to the first turnpoint (although with a 15 kt headwind). The second leg to HusBos should be good although around HusBos it may be overconvected. Conditions will weaken before 15:00, by which time conditions are expected to be weak with climbs to 3500 foot max. We're expecting a 15 knot headwind on the first leg, tailwind on the others.

To begin, 'Hunt' for a thermal, and select 'Start Task' at the top of a climb when you're ready to go. There is more help and a detailed discussion of the simulator in the help file.
