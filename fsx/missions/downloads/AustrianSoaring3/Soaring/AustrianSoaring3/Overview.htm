Welcome to day 3 in the Austrian Soaring competition here at Zell am See.

The special airspace restriction across the whole area to 9000 feet continues from yesterday. If you exceed this you will FAIL the mission.

Make a good start above the airfield (maximum start height 8000 feet MSL) and navigate via each waypoint to return to the airfield. The task takes you South-East, then North, then West and back home.

The mission begins in flight, approaching the start line. Your ground crew will monitor your progress via radio telemetry.

The wind is 16 knots from the North-East, so keep to the ridges on the South or West sides of the valleys and avoid the sink and curlover in the lee of upwind hills. Do not head off into unlandable high areas unless you are certain of an available escape route and confident of lift.

Before flying this mission, fly the FSX missions "Austrian Alpine Soaring Course" and "Austrian Soaring - day 2"
