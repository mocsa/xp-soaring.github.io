Suggested route overview

Notes

  1. Head south from the start to get onto the local ridge.
  2. Slow up and gain a little height on the ridge as you approach Mill Creek.
  3. After Mill Creek, make a small UPWIND jump to the ridge to the north and follow it until exactly upwind of Allenville in the valley.
  4. Cross downwind across the valley passing through the Allenville turnpoint.
  5. Get high above the ridge on the approach to Three Barns so you can transition to a ridge to the north and follow that to the turnpoint.
  6. Get high again on the way back from Three Barns, so you can make the downwind jump back onto the Mifflin south ridge. Follow the ridge back to just east of Mifflin for a safe into-wind final glide.
