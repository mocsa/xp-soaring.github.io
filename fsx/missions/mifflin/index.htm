Mifflin FSX Missions: The Tutorial

Introduction

This tutorial is written as a guide to help you get the most out of the Mifflin Soaring Competition missions for FSX.

As a quick overview, these missions are:

  1. Day 1, 172km: Mifflin - Mill Creek - Allenville - Three Barns - Mifflin. A ridge run around the local Mifflin valley.
  2. Day 2, XXXkm: Mifflin - Spruce Creek Tunnels - Blair County Airport - Penns Cave Airport - Mifflin.
  3. Day 3, 232km: Mifflin - Selinsgrove Airport - Orbisonia - Mifflin.
  4. Day 4, 227km: Mifflin - Kettle Reservoir - Lock Haven Airport - Mifflin.
  5. Day 5, 531km: Mifflin - Cumberland Airport - Lock Haven Airport - Kettle Reservoir - Mifflin.
