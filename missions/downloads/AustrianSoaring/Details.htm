Navigation

Fly around the course as quickly as possible. Altitude is at your discretion.

The route has been programmed into the GPS (press SHIFT+3 to display/hide).

There are 11 turnpoints you must fly to in sequence between the start and finish lines. After crossing the finish line, land at Zell Am See Airport (LOWZ).

You can navigate in this mission by following the Mission Compass and Mission Pointer. For a more challenging experience, you can turn the Mission Compass and Mission Pointer off (press U).

Communications

Your ground crew will communicate with you via radio. Your callsign is "Zulu Hotel."
