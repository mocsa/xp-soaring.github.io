Pye Green Tower for Flight Simulator (52 deg 43.7099 N, 2 deg 1.1868 W)

Pye Green Tower scenery for FSX can be downloaded here: Pye Green Tower v1_2.zip

To install: drag the "Pye Green Tower" folder into your "FSXBASE\Addon Scenery" folder, and then add the scenery to your FSX scenery library via "SETTINGS" - "Scenery Library..." - "Add Area..."
